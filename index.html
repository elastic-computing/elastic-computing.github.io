Overview

Advancements in the areas of Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, and hybrid Human-Computer systems have made feasible the creation of a highly integrated human-machine world. The concept of elasticity plays a crucial role in fulfilling this vision, enabling systems to address various requirements reflecting performance, security, or business concerns. In this book introduce concepts characterizing elastic computing and techniques for designing and managing elastic cloud systems. We follow the complete lifecycle of elastic systems, from design-time to operations management, and provide solutions to particular problems encountered at each of these phases. Our solutions are accompanied by hands-on exercises and examples.
